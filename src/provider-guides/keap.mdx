## What's supported

### Supported actions

This connector supports:
- [Read Actions](/read-actions), including full historic backfill and incremental read for some.
- [Write Actions](/write-actions).
- [Proxy Actions](/proxy-actions), using the base URL `https://api.infusionsoft.com`.

The Keap connector supports writing to and reading from the following objects:
- [affiliates](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Affiliate/operation/listAffiliatesUsingGET) (no update)
- [appointments](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Appointment/operation/listAppointmentsUsingGET) (incremental read)
- [companies](https://developer.keap.com/docs/restv2/#tag/Company/operation/listCompaniesUsingGET_1)
- [contacts](https://developer.keap.com/docs/restv2/#tag/Contact/operation/listContactsUsingGET_1) (incremental read)
- [emails](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Email/operation/listEmailsUsingGET) (no update)
- [files](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/File/operation/listFilesUsingGET)
- [hooks](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/REST-Hooks/operation/list_stored_hook_subscriptions)
- [notes](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Note/operation/listNotesUsingGET)
- [opportunities](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Opportunity/operation/listOpportunitiesUsingGET)
- [orders](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/E-Commerce/operation/listOrdersUsingGET) (incremental read, create, no update)
- [products](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Product/operation/listProductsUsingGET)
- [subscriptions](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/E-Commerce/operation/listSubscriptionsUsingGET)  (no update)
- [tag_categories](https://developer.keap.com/docs/restv2/#tag/Tags/operation/listTagCategoriesUsingGET)
- [tags](https://developer.keap.com/docs/restv2/#tag/Tags/operation/listTagsUsingGET_1)
- [tasks](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Task/operation/listTasksUsingGET) (incremental read)
- [users](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Users/operation/listUsersUsingGET) (no update)

The Keap connector only supports reading from the following objects:
- [campaigns](https://developer.keap.com/docs/restv2/#tag/Campaign/operation/listCampaignsUsingGET_1)
- [commissions](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Affiliate/operation/listCommissionsUsingGET) (incremental read)
- [merchants](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Merchant/operation/getMerchantAccountsUsingGET)
- [programs](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Affiliate/operation/listProgramsUsingGET)
- [redirectlinks](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Affiliate/operation/listAffiliateRedirectLinksUsingGET)
- [stage_pipeline](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/Opportunity/operation/listOpportunityStagePipelinesUsingGET)
- [transactions](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/docs/rest/#tag/E-Commerce/operation/listTransactionsUsingGET) (incremental read)

Custom fields are returned just like any other field on objects that support them.

## Before you get started

To integrate Keap with Ampersand, you will need a [Keap Developer Account](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/).

Once your account is created, you'll need to create an app in Keap and obtain the following credentials from your app:

- App ID
- Secret

You will then use these credentials to connect your application to Ampersand.

### Create a Keap account

Here's how you can sign up for a Keap account:

- Go to the [Keap Developer Portal](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/).
- Sign up using your preferred method and complete any necessary verification.

### Creating a Keap app

Follow the steps below to create a Keap app and add the Ampersand redirect URL.

1. Log in to your [Keap Developer Portal](https://developer.infusionsoft.com/).

2. Click **Apps** from your account drop-down menu.

3. Enter the following app details:

- **App Name**: The name of your app.
- **App Description**: A brief description of your app and its purpose.

4. Select **Enable** button for the given Keap API.
5. Click **Save**.

![Keap App Creation](/images/provider-guides/84c0e13-keap1.gif)

You will find the **App ID** and **Secret** in the app details. Note these credentials, as you will need them to connect your app to Ampersand.

## Add your Keap app info to Ampersand

1. Log in to your [Ampersand Dashboard](https://dashboard.withampersand.com).

2. Select the project where you want to create a Keap integration.

   ![Ampersand Project Selection](/images/provider-guides/dd47b7a-Ampersand.png)

3. Select **Provider Apps**.

4. Select _Keap_ from the **Provider** list.

5. Enter the previously obtained **App ID** in the **Client ID** field and **Secret** in the **Client Secret** field.

   ![Ampersand Integration](/images/provider-guides/f474236-keap2.gif)

6. Click **Save Changes**.
