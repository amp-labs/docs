## Checklist before installing a Zoho integration

Complete these steps before installing the Zoho integration to ensure a smooth setup process.

### 1. Register a Server-Based Application

1. Navigate to the [Zoho Developer Console](https://api-console.zoho.com/)
2. Click **GET STARTED**
   ![Zoho Get Started](/images/customer-guides/zoho-get-started.png)

3. Select **Server-based Applications** as your client type and click **CREATE NOW**
   ![Select Client Type](/images/customer-guides/zoho-select-client.png)

4. Fill in the application details:
   - **Application Name**: Your preferred name
   - **Homepage URL**: Your application's URL
   - **Redirect URI**: `https://api.withampersand.com/callbacks/v1/oauth`
   ![Application Details](/images/customer-guides/zoho-app-details.png)

5. Click **Create** to proceed to the Client Secrets tab, within this page you can observe the **client ID** and **client Secret**
   ![Client Secrets](/images/customer-guides/zoho-client-secrets.png)

6. Navigate to the **Settings** tab and enable **Use the same OAuth credentials for all data centers**
   ![Multi-Datacenter Settings](/images/customer-guides/zoho-multi-dc.png)

For additional guidance, refer to [Zoho's official documentation](https://www.zoho.com/crm/developer/docs/api/v8/register-client.html).
